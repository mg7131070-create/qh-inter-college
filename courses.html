Academic Programs

We offer intermediate education in the following streams.

Science Stream

The Science stream is designed for students interested in engineering, medical, and research-oriented careers. Practical lab work is an essential part of this stream.

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Mathematics or Biology
  • Computer Science
  • English

Commerce Stream

The Commerce stream prepares students for careers in business, finance, accounting, and economics.

  • Accountancy
  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • Mathematics or Hindi
  • English

Humanities (Arts)

The Humanities stream focuses on social sciences and helps students develop analytical, critical thinking, and communication skills.

  • History
  • Geography
  • Political Science
  • Sociology
  • English or Hindi
