Admissions

Admission Process for the Academic Session 2024–25

Eligibility Criteria

  • Class 11: Students must have passed Class 10 with the required subjects.
  • Merit Based: Admissions are granted on merit and availability of seats.
  • Transfer Students: Valid Transfer Certificate (TC) is mandatory.

Required Documents

  • Class 10 Marksheet (Photocopy)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Aadhaar Card (Photocopy)
  • Two Passport Size Photographs

How to Apply

  1. Visit the college office during working hours.
  2. Collect the admission form and prospectus.
  3. Fill the form carefully with correct details.
  4. Attach all required documents.
  5. Submit the form before the last date.

Important Notes

Admissions are strictly based on merit. Submission of the application does not guarantee admission. The decision of the admission committee will be final.
