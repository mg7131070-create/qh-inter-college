About Q.H. Inter College

A tradition of excellence in education.

Our History

Q.H. Inter College was established with the vision of providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. Over the years, the college has grown into a respected institution known for discipline, academic excellence, and moral values.

Our Mission

Our mission is to nurture young minds by providing a supportive learning environment that encourages intellectual growth, critical thinking, and responsible citizenship.

Our Vision

We aim to be recognized as a leading educational institution that prepares students for higher education and future careers, while instilling strong ethical values.
